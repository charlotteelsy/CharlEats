Pesto Gnocchi

This is my take on a vegan, healthy version of Pizza Express' Pollo Pesto. Takes less than half an hour to make so it's perfect for a quick dinner!

To make for four people you will need:

500g of Gnocchi

1/2 a pot of Pesto

1 Red Onion

4 cloves of Garlic

1 Punnet of Mushrooms

250g of Spinach

1 tsp of Basil

1 tbsp of Cream Cheese

Instructions:

Add some oil to a pan and add your mushrooms, garlic and onion. Fry this on a medium heat for 10 minutes.
Whilst this is cooking add your gnocchi to a pan of boiling water and boil for 5 minutes.
Once the Gnocchi is cooked, add to the frying pan with the vegetables, and add the pesto, cream cheese, spinach and basil. Cook on a low heat for 5 more minutes, stirring regularly, until the spinach has wilted. You're then ready to plate up and tuck in!
