Vegan Stirfry

This is a really delicious dish. It's completely plantbased and takes less than 30 minutes to whip up!

To make for four people you will need:

4 nests of rice noodles

450g of Tofu (my favourite is the Tofoo brand), cut up into squares

400g of Frozen Stirfry Veg

12 Mushrooms, sliced

4 Spring Onions, cut into small pieces

4 Garlic Cloves, diced

3 Tablespoons of Soy Sauce

Sesame Oil

Sriracha

1 tsp paprika

1 tsp chinese five spice

Instructions:

First, add the sesame oil to a frying pan then add your tofu, and add the paprika, five spice and garlic and ensure the tofu is well coated. Fry this on a high heat for 10 minutes until the tofu starts browning.
Then, add the mushrooms and the stirfry veg. Whilst this is cooking, add your rice noodles to a pan of boiling water for 5 minutes.
Once the noodles are cooked, add them to the pan with the vegetables and tofu and add the soy sauce, and continue to cook for a few more minutes. Following this, plate up your food, add the spring onions and top with Sriracha (if you like spice)!
