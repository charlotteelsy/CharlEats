Summer Salad

This salad is very summery and light in flavours, but also very filling and won't leave you feeling hungry like other salads might.

To make for four people you will need:

250g Quinoa (the Merchant's Quinoa works well)

100g Pomegranite Seeds

100g Feta

1 Red Pepper (sweet peppers work best but bell peppers also work)

2 Spring Onions

Chopped Corriander and Parsley (to your taste)

For the dressing - Olive Oil and Lemon Juice (to your taste)

Extra toppings - vegan chicken and vegan chorizo

Instructions:

First you need to cook your vegan chicken and chorizo in a frying pan in a bit of oil if you are including them. Whilst these are cooking, cut up all your other ingredients and mix together in a big bowl. Make the dressing, before adding the chicken and chorizo and pour the dressing over the salad to finish!
